The Science of Preservation.

UV & PET Lamination

Decades of display, zero compromise. Our UV and PET laminates are sourced from professional-grade suppliers and applied with cold-press precision to eliminate bubbles and ensure perfect adhesion — even on canvas.

UV Lamination — Blocks 98% of UV radiation. Colours stay vivid for 20+ years indoors.
PET Lamination — Industrial-grade scratch and moisture resistance. Ideal for high-traffic areas, offices, and retail.
Matte or Gloss Finish — Choose the surface texture that suits your interior and print type.

Liquid Resin Encapsulation

A flawless, modern aesthetic reserved for collectors and corporates who want the absolute best. We pour food-safe, crystal-clear liquid resin over your prints and allow it to cure under controlled conditions into a thick, permanent, glass-like shell that elevates any photograph into a three-dimensional art object.

Crystal-Clear Depth — Adds 3–5mm of optical-grade depth to your print.
UV-Stable Resin — Will not yellow or crack under normal indoor conditions for decades.
Frameless or Framed — Stunning standalone, or combined with our gallery frames for a dual luxury finish.

Our Story

Four Decades of Trust at Ganga Nagar Traffic.

We started in 1984 with a simple promise: craftsmanship over shortcuts. What began as a modest framing shop at Ganga Nagar Traffic has grown, through word of mouth and uncompromising quality alone, into Bhubaneswar's most trusted name in lamination and custom framing.

Clients who brought us their wedding photographs in the 1990s are now returning to frame their children's graduation portraits — and soon, their grandchildren's. That continuity is not a marketing line. It is the only measure of quality we have ever cared about.
